Capabilities

Built for the Way You Write

Every feature exists because ChoiceScript authors asked for it — and couldn't find it anywhere else.

Live Error Checking

Errors surface as you type — 400ms after you stop — with exact line numbers and fixes. Like Grammarly, but for ChoiceScript logic.

Acts & Chapters

Organise your scenes into acts visually. Drag scenes between chapters. See your story's architecture at a glance.

Character Tracker

Every character detected across your scenes — their appearances, variables, and scene count — automatically, without tagging anything.

Word Count Goals

Set a target. Track progress with a live bar. Daily, weekly, or full-project periods — your pace, your rules.

Focus / Write Mode

One keypress collapses everything but the words. Full-screen, distraction-free writing with the editor expanded edge-to-edge.

Export Ready

One click packages your entire project — scenes, images, README — into a .zip ready to submit to Choice of Games or host yourself.
